About Trish Kennedy

I am a versatile creative currently residing in Houston, Texas, and Lagos, Nigeria. From a young age, I have had a passion for fashion and photography, which inspired me to pursue a career as a fashion designer. Throughout the years, I have explored various fields, but I always knew I would find my way back to the arts.

My creative journey began in 2017 when I started modeling, a path I naturally embraced. In 2020, I transitioned into styling and fashion design, as I frequently found myself on set surrounded by an abundance of clothing. This allowed me to assist in styling both myself and other models. Eventually, I started creative directing my own photo shoots and bringing other people's concepts to life. This transition has been incredibly rewarding, as I genuinely enjoy helping others realize their creative visions and expectations.

I never anticipated that my curiosity for creativity would lead me this far, but it certainly has. Over the past eight years, I have experienced and accomplished so much, and I am excited to see what the future has in store for me.
