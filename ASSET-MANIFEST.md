## IMAGE SPECIFICATIONS & OPTIMIZATION

### General Guidelines
- **Maximum file size**: 500KB per image (portfolio images)
- **Hero image**: Can be up to 800KB if necessary for quality
- **Format**: JPG for photographs, PNG for graphics with transparency, SVG for logos/icons
- **Color space**: sRGB
- **Resolution**: 72 DPI (web standard)
- **Compression**: Use tools like TinyPNG, ImageOptim, or Squoosh

### Aspect Ratios
- **Portrait spreads**: 3:4 (1800x2400px)
- **Landscape spreads**: 4:3 (2400x1800px)
- **Hero background**: 3:2 or 16:9 (flexible based on composition)
- **Square variants**: 1:1 (1800x1800px) for social media reuse

### Treatment Guidelines
- High contrast where appropriate (editorial look)
- Optional: Film grain texture (can be added via CSS)
- Optional: Subtle vignette on hero image
- Grayscale/B&W conversions should be professional grade
- Ensure faces and key details are sharp and well-lit
